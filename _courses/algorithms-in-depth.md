## Stop memorising. Start deriving.

Interview prep teaches you to recognise problems. This course teaches you to **solve ones you've never seen** — by internalising the handful of design paradigms that generate most known algorithms, and the proof techniques that show they work.

## Who this is for

- Engineers preparing for hard technical roles who want true understanding
- CS students who want depth beyond their coursework
- Anyone who can implement a data structure but can't yet *prove* their algorithm is correct

## Prerequisites

Familiarity with basic data structures (arrays, trees, hash tables) and comfort reading pseudocode.
