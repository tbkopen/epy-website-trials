{%- comment -%} Featured-course widget (blog index sidebar). The course shown is driven by the `featured_course` variable (a course uid) in _pages/site-variables.md — change that uid to feature a different course; this widget then shows that course's details and links to its external `course-link`. The pages-collection lookup mirrors notification.html (a direct site.pages lookup collides with Jekyll's built-in pages list). The external-vs-internal link handling mirrors course-card.html: a `course-link` containing "://" opens in a new tab; otherwise it's a baseurl-safe internal link.
