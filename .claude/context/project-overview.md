## Purpose

Elastropy is a personal blog-first website that uses high-quality content (especially math/technical posts) to build an audience and convert readers into paying course students.

The site has two layers:
1. **Public brand layer** — blog posts, about page, free resources
2. **Commercial layer** — course catalog, sales pages, purchase flow

---

## Business Model

| Stage | Mechanism |
|-------|-----------|
| Discovery | Organic search, social sharing of blog posts |
| Engagement | Blog comments (Remark42), email newsletter |
| Conversion | CTAs in blog posts → course sales pages |
| Retention | Email sequences, new content, community |

Revenue comes entirely from course sales. Blog content is free and serves as the trust-building medium.

## Content Strategy

- Blog posts are the primary content type — published regularly
- Posts should be genuinely educational, not just promotional
- Math-heavy posts signal credibility and attract a serious audience
- Each post should naturally connect to one or more courses via a bottom-of-post CTA
- A newsletter opt-in should appear on every post page
